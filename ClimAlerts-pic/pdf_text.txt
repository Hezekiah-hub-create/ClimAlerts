ClimAlerts UI Components & Feature Explanations
Introduction
This document explains the purpose, meaning, and relevance of the UI components designed for the
ClimAlerts system.
ClimAlerts is an intelligent climate-health monitoring platform focused on predicting disease outbreaks in
the Volta Region using environmental and health data.
The UI components are designed to simulate a real-world public health monitoring system used by health
authorities, analysts, and administrators.
1. Dashboard UI
Purpose
The Dashboard acts as the main control center of the system.
It gives administrators and health officials a quick overview of:
current outbreak risks
environmental conditions
active alerts
AI recommendations
system activity
Dashboard Components
Overview Statistics Cards
These cards summarize important health and climate information.
Examples:

3. Risk Map UI
Purpose
The Risk Map visually displays outbreak risk levels across districts in the Volta Region.
Risk Map Components
District Heatmap
Uses colors to represent outbreak severity.
Color Meaning:
Green = Low Risk
Yellow = Moderate Risk
Orange = High Risk
Red = Critical Risk
Relevance
Allows officials to quickly identify dangerous districts.
Volta Lake Overlay
Displays the Volta Lake on the map.
Relevance
Important because nearby water bodies influence mosquito breeding and disease spread.
District Details Panel
Shows detailed information for the selected district.
